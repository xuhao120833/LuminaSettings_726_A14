Projector Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

·

We value the importance of privacy protection. This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") is intended to clearly inform you how our projector products collect, use, store, and process device-related information.

We solemnly promise: we will not collect or process any information that can directly or indirectly identify you personally (such as name, contact information, location information, browsing history, or personal usage habits), unless we have obtained your explicit consent.

2. Types of Information We Collect and Purpose

2.1 Device Feature Information (Required for Basic Services)

To provide core device services, we automatically collect the following device feature information as follows:

For Online Software Upgrade Service (OTA):

  • Information collected: Device serial number (SN), MAC address, current firmware version, device model, hardware configuration.
  • Purpose of collection: This information is the basis for OTA service operation. By identifying the device's unique serial number and firmware version, we can accurately determine your device model and current software status, thereby precisely delivering matching and secure software updates and patches.

For App Market Services:

  • Information collected: Device channel number, region identifier, system version, and application installation list summary (including only app package names and versions, excluding usage data).
  • Purpose of collection: Used to display applications compatible with your device model and region, and to push corresponding application update notifications, ensuring you receive available and stable application resources.

2.2 Device Operation Log Information (Collected with Your Consent)

To provide better technical support, when a device encounters an exception or malfunction, we will request your consent to collect the following device operation log information:

  • Log content: May include error codes, crash reports, performance data, operation status of specific functional modules, and other non-personal technical information.
  • Collection method and premise: This information will not be uploaded automatically. It will only be prompted when the device detects a specific exception or you actively report an issue, and your consent will be requested. You have the right to agree or refuse to submit.
  • Purpose of collection: This information is strictly used for technical analysis, fault diagnosis, troubleshooting, and product service improvement. It helps us locate and solve issues encountered by your device more quickly and accurately, thereby optimizing product stability and user experience.

2.3 Nature of Information

All the above information constitutes the device's "identity markers", technical features, and operational status information. This information is only used to associate and manage the device itself and its performance, and is not related to any specific user account or personal identity information. We cannot and will not use this information to identify or associate with specific individuals.

3. Information Storage and Retention Period

3.1 Storage Method

Collected device feature information and operation log information submitted with your consent will be securely encrypted and stored on our servers.

3.2 Retention Period

  • Device feature information: To achieve full lifecycle management of the device (including long-term technical support, security updates, and compliance requirements), unless the device is physically recycled or permanently decommissioned, we will retain these device feature records on the server. This ensures that even after long-term use, we can still provide necessary security updates and technical support for the device.
  • Device operation log information: This information will be anonymized or securely deleted within a reasonable period after completing fault analysis and service improvement purposes (usually no more than 6 months), unless otherwise required by laws or regulations.

3.3 Information Disposal

If the device is recycled or you actively contact us to request deletion of device information, we will verify device ownership and remove the associated records from the active database according to internal procedures. Some information may be archived for a limited period due to legal or compliance requirements.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We will never sell, rent, or otherwise commercially share your device information.

We may only share necessary information with partners under the following strictly limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Our commissioned cloud services and technical analysis partners (such as OTA distribution providers, log analysis platforms) may only access information when performing services on our behalf, and are bound by strict contractual obligations prohibiting the use of information for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: When we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal procedures, government mandatory requirements, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of ourselves and users.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, device information may be part of the transferred assets, but the recipient must continue to comply with this Policy.

5. Your Choices and Control

  • Device Feature Information: To ensure the normal operation of basic services such as OTA and App Market, the collection of such information is necessary and cannot be turned off individually. If you do not agree, it may affect the normal use of related core functions.
  • Device Operation Log Information: You have full control. You can view and manage the consent status for log reporting at any time in Device Settings > About > Privacy Settings (or a similar path). You can turn off this feature at any time and will no longer be prompted for future submissions. You can also choose to make individual decisions each time you are prompted.
  • Viewing and Deleting Information: If you wish to view the feature information we have stored about your device, or request deletion of submitted log information, please contact us as described in Section 8. We will respond in accordance with laws, regulations, and this Policy after verifying device ownership.

6. Data Security

We adopt industry-standard technical and management measures to protect device information, including but not limited to:

  • Using encryption technology (such as TLS/SSL) during data transmission.
  • Encrypting stored data and controlling access.
  • Conducting regular security audits and vulnerability scans.
  • Limiting access to authorized technical personnel who have a strict confidentiality obligation.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated version will be notified in the projector system settings and published on our official website, while updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy. For major changes, we will provide more prominent notice.
